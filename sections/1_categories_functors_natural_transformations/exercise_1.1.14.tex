\subsection{}

The composite of two morphisms
\[
	(f_1, g_1) \colon (A_0, B_0) \to (A_1, B_1) \,,
	\quad
	(f_2, g_2) \colon (A_1, B_1) \to (A_2, B_2)
\]
in~$\cat{A} × \cat{B}$ is given by
\[
	(f_2, g_2) ∘ (f_1, g_1)
	≔
	(f_2 ∘ f_1, g_2 ∘ g_1) \,.
\]
The identity morphism of an object~$(A, B)$ of~$\cat{A} × \cat{B}$ is given by
\[
	\id_{(A, B)}
	=
	(\id_A, \id_B) \,.
\]
