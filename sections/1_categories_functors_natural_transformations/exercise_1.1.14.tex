\subsection{}

The composite of two morphisms
\[
	(f, g) \colon (A, B) \to (A', B') \,,
	\quad
	(f', g') \colon (A', B') \to (A'', B'')
\]
in~$\cat{A} × \cat{B}$ is given by
\[
	(f', g') ∘ (f, g)
	≔
	(f' ∘ f, g' ∘ g) \,.
\]
The identity morphism of an object~$(A, B)$ of~$\cat{A} × \cat{B}$ is then given by~$(\id_A, \id_B)$, i.e.,
\[
	\id_{(A, B)}
	=
	(\id_A, \id_B) \,.
\]
