\subsubsection{}

For any two sets~$A$ and~$B$ we consider the evaluation map
\[
	α_{AB}
	\colon
	A × B^A \to B \,,
	\quad
	(a, f) \mapsto f(a) \,.
\]



\subsubsection{}

For any two sets~$A$ and~$B$ we consider the map
\[
	β_{AB}
	\colon
	A \to B^{B^A} \,,
	\quad
	a \mapsto [f \mapsto f(a)] \,.
\]
